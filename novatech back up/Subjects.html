Our Subjects

Choose from our comprehensive range of subjects designed to equip you with essential skills for your future.

Mathematics

Master the language of numbers and develop critical problem-solving skills essential for STEM careers and everyday life.

Key Topics:

  • Algebra and equations - Solving linear, quadratic and simultaneous equations
  • Functions and graphs - Understanding various function types and their graphical representations
  • Trigonometry - Mastering trigonometric functions, identities and applications
  • Calculus - Introduction to differentiation and integration concepts
  • Probability and statistics - Data analysis, probability distributions and statistical inference

Our mathematics program focuses on developing logical thinking and analytical skills essential for various career paths. We emphasize practical applications of mathematical concepts through real-world problem solving. Our experienced instructors provide personalized attention to ensure each student builds confidence in their mathematical abilities.

Physical Science

Explore the fundamental principles of physics and chemistry that govern our universe through hands-on experiments.

Key Topics:

  • Mechanics and motion - Newton's laws, kinematics, and energy transformations
  • Electricity and magnetism - Circuits, electromagnetism and applications
  • Chemical reactions - Stoichiometry, reaction rates and equilibrium
  • Atomic structure - Quantum theory, periodic trends and chemical bonding
  • Energy and thermodynamics - Laws of thermodynamics, heat transfer

Our physical science program combines theoretical knowledge with practical experiments to foster scientific thinking. Students gain access to our well-equipped laboratory facilities where they can test theories and observe phenomena firsthand. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for university-level science courses while making complex concepts accessible through interactive learning methods.

CAT (Computation Application Technology)

Develop essential digital literacy and computer application skills for the modern workplace and academic success.

Key Topics:

  • Word processing and spreadsheets - Advanced document formatting and data analysis
  • Database management - Designing, querying and maintaining databases
  • Internet and networking - Web technologies and network fundamentals
  • Basic programming concepts - Introduction to algorithms and coding principles
  • Digital citizenship and ethics - Responsible technology use and cybersecurity

Our CAT program equips students with practical technology skills essential for academic and professional success. We focus on developing competencies that are directly applicable in modern workplaces, from creating professional documents to analyzing data. Students work with industry-standard software and gain exposure to emerging technologies, preparing them for both further studies and immediate employment opportunities in our digital world.

English

Enhance your communication skills and develop critical thinking through language mastery and literary analysis.

Key Topics:

  • Reading comprehension - Analyzing complex texts and extracting key information
  • Writing techniques - Crafting essays, reports and creative pieces
  • Grammar and vocabulary - Mastering language structures and expanding lexicon
  • Oral communication - Developing confident public speaking and presentation skills
  • Literary analysis - Interpreting various genres and understanding literary devices

Our English program focuses on developing both practical communication skills and appreciation for literature. Students engage with a diverse range of texts from different cultures and historical periods, fostering critical thinking and cultural awareness. We emphasize the development of articulate written and spoken expression, preparing students for academic writing at tertiary level and effective communication in professional contexts. Small class sizes ensure personalized feedback to help each student refine their language skills.
